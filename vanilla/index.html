Tribute to Nikola Tesla

Nikola Tesla, circa 1890

Timeline

  • 1856: Nikola Tesla was born on July 10 in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (modern-day Croatia).
  • 1884: Tesla arrived in the United States with little money and a letter of introduction to famed inventor and business mogul Thomas Edison. He began working for Edison but the two would soon part ways.
  • 1888: Tesla developed the AC electrical supply system that included a motor and transformer, and that Westinghouse Electric would ultimately use to power the nation.
  • 1891: Tesla became a naturalized citizen of the United States.
  • 1895: Tesla designed the first hydroelectric power plant in Niagara Falls.
  • 1943: Nikola Tesla died alone in his room at the New Yorker Hotel on January 7, 1943, at the age of 86.
