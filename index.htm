Mahatma Gandhi

"Be the change that you wish to see in the world." - Mahatma Gandhi

Timeline

  • 1869: Born on October 2, 1869, in Porbandar, British India.
  • 1888: Traveled to London to study law.
  • 1893: Traveled to South Africa, where he experienced racial discrimination and began his activism.
  • 1915: Returned to India and joined the Indian National Congress.
  • 1947: India gained independence from British rule.
  • 1948: Assassinated on January 30, 1948.
